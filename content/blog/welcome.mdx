Side projects rarely fail because people lack ambition. They fade because life gets loud — and most tools import the wrong kind of urgency.

SomedayPM is our answer: **gentle structure**, **honest language**, and a visual system that feels more like a quiet desk than a command center.

## What you'll find here

- Short notes on product decisions
- Behind-the-scenes on beta feedback
- Occasional essays on creative work without burnout

This is an MVP journal: markdown files in the repo, no CMS. If a post resonates, we'd love to hear from you on the [contact](/contact) page.
